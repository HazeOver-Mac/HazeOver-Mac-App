## Overview  

**HazeOver for Mac** transforms your workspace into a calmer, more focused environment by intelligently dimming background windows while keeping your active one fully visible. It’s the perfect productivity companion for users who multitask but need to minimize distractions to stay efficient.  

By automatically adjusting lighting and contrast, HazeOver helps your eyes naturally gravitate toward the active application. Whether you’re writing, editing, coding, or simply browsing, this tool ensures that your attention stays centered on what really matters.  

The app is designed to blend perfectly with macOS — no clutter, no complexity, just smooth and adaptive functionality. You can easily tweak dimming levels, switch between modes, and manage multiple screens with consistent focus effects. **HazeOver** supports both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs, providing a seamless experience with zero performance drag.  

Ideal for professionals, students, and creatives alike, **HazeOver** reduces visual noise and mental fatigue, allowing you to work longer and more comfortably. Once you start using it, you’ll wonder how you ever managed your Mac desktop without it.
